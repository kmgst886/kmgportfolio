Intercultural Blog Website

This blog website was created for a project in my Intercultural Communications class. The goal was to explore and share cultural insights and perspectives in a visually engaging and accessible way.

I designed the layout, constructed the structure from scratch, and incorporated modern web effects such as smooth scrolling, hover transitions, and responsive components. The goal was to reflect the values of communication, clarity, and diversity.

Design & Development Process

Planning & Research

I analyzed cultural blog sites and studied best practices for readability, visual hierarchy, and inclusivity. I also defined content categories that reflect intercultural themes such as identity, language, and global issues.

Layout & UX Design

Using HTML and CSS, I built a clean, responsive layout with a focus on accessibility. Elements like featured post banners, category navigation, and article cards were designed for intuitive interaction.

Interactive Effects

To enhance user experience, I incorporated scroll-based animations, hover effects, and a dynamic header using basic JavaScript. These effects added polish without compromising performance.
