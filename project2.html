Shipping Craze

Shipping craze is a board game that allows players to enjoy the unboxing sensation and the enjoyment of buying products that they would not normally buy without spending a penny or their own money.

My role in this project was the creation and full developemnt of the product, and I was responsible for all the designs and models.
