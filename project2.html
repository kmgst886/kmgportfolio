Shipping Craze

Shipping Craze is a chaotic and hilarious board game where players race to fulfill surprise orders using mystery boxes — without spending a penny of real money. From quirky items to clever combos, it’s all about strategy, luck, and speed!

As the sole designer, I developed the entire game from concept to prototype: game rules, artwork, tokens, and testing strategies.

Development Process

Game Concept & Mechanics

Inspired by the thrill of online unboxing and bargain hunting, I built core mechanics around mystery item reveals, combo-building, and resource management. Themes of chaos and humor guided early brainstorming.

Prototyping

I created the first prototype using printable cards and household items. Initial playtests helped refine the pacing and objectives. I developed item decks, order cards, and player dashboards to clarify turns and scoring.

Playtesting & Balancing

Repeated sessions with players aged 10+ revealed key improvements: reducing downtime, simplifying symbols, and reworking bonus rounds. Feedback was implemented in each version to strengthen both fun and fairness.
