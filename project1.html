Creative Process

The book was written over 3-4 Months with a focus on playful rhyme and visual rhythm. I used Procreate for illustration and stock images as references for my sketches.

The Objective of this story is not only for it to bring kids a sense of joy and entertainment, but it is also to teach kids a life lesson of not anyone they just meet us their friend.
